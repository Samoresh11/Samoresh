Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my services and process

How long does it take to complete a website?
The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. Starter packages typically take 2 weeks, Professional packages take 3 weeks, and Enterprise packages take 6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?
Yes! All packages include initial support (30-180 days depending on the package). I also offer ongoing maintenance plans starting at $200/month that include security updates, content updates, and technical support.
What technologies do you use?
I use modern, industry-standard technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Node.js, and various CMS platforms. The specific technology stack will be chosen based on your project requirements and goals.
Do you work with clients internationally?
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am comfortable with remote collaboration. I use modern communication tools and project management systems to ensure smooth communication regardless of time zones.
What's your payment structure?
I typically work with a 50% deposit to start the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, PayPal, and major credit cards.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! All websites I create are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. I use a mobile-first approach to ensure your site looks and works perfectly on all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers.
